Smoked Salmon Toast w/
Mashed Chickpea & Kale

Packed with flavor and protein, ideal for busy weeknights or an easy high-protein breakfast. Contains dairy and gluten. Recipe sourced from Kuri app.

  • Cooking Time

    Ready in 20-30 minutes

  • Nutrition Per Serving

    381 kcal

    Carbs 27g (incl. 3g sugar)
    Proteins 17g
    Fats 20g (incl. 2g saturated)
    Fibers 6g

  • Special Equipment

    Blender
    Toaster (or a pan)

    •

    Ingredients

    Portions for 3

    • Kale

      • 1 cup

    • Carrot

      • 1/2 cup sliced

    • Chickpeas

      • 8 oz cooked

    • Scallion

      • 1

    • Smoked salmon

      • 3 slices

    • Mayonnaise

      • 21/2 tbsp to taste

    • Yellow mustard

      • 1 tsp

    • Flat leaf parsley

      • a small handful

    • Nutritional yeast

      • 1 tbsp

    • Lemon juice

      • 2 tsp

    • Curry powder

      • 1/4 tsp

    • Ground cumin

      • 1/4 tsp

    • Greek Yogurt

      • 3 tbsp

    • Dill

      • a small handful chopped

    • Whole wheat bread slices

      • 3

    • Olive oil

      • 2 tsp

    • Salt
    • Ground pepper

    Instructions

    1. Place the kale and carrot into a blender and blend until they are finely chopped.
    2. Add the chickpeas, nutritional yeast, mayonnaise, yellow mustard, flat leaf parsley, scallion, lemon juice, ground cumin, and curry powder to the blender. Season with ground pepper and blend until the mixture is uniform but still has some small, crunchy pieces.
    3. In a separate bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, chopped dill, and olive oil. Season with salt and ground pepper.
    4. Toast the whole wheat bread slices. Once toasted, spread a generous amount of the chickpea and kale mixture onto each slice.
    5. Top each slice with a piece of smoked salmon and finish with a dollop of the dill yogurt. Enjoy your meal!
